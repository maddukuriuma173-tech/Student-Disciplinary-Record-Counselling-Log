# Problem Statement & Abstract
**Project**: Student Disciplinary Record & Counselling Log  
**Institution**: Sri Gowthami Educational Institutions  

---

## 1. Problem Statement
Sri Gowthami Educational Institutions, a multi-campus education group, currently handles student misconduct incidents, counselling sessions, parent meetings, and improvement action plans without a dedicated digital system. 

At present, managing student discipline depends on phone calls, WhatsApp messages, scattered spreadsheets, and manual paper-based follow-ups. This results in:
* **Inefficiency & Delays**: Data entries are scattered across campuses, leading to communication lag, errors, and loss of records.
* **Lack of Centralized Tracking**: Management cannot see real-time student discipline statuses, making it impossible to identify patterns of repeat misconduct.
* **Repetitive Manual Overhead**: Compiling records from multiple campuses requires significant manual effort and email chains, wasting valuable administrative hours.
* **Absence of Actionable Insights**: Without a structured workflow, the school cannot proactively intervene to support students before their behavioral issues escalate.
* **Inadequate Legal/Administrative Protection**: Complete audit histories and parent meeting logs are not legally structured, leaving the institution vulnerable during legal or regulatory reviews.

---

## 2. Abstract
The **Student Disciplinary Record & Counselling Log** is an automated, web-based prototype designed to centralize and streamline disciplinary tracking. 

The system enables school staff to input misconduct details, log counselling sessions and parent-teacher meetings, and define clear student improvement action plans. It incorporates a **core business logic engine** that automatically assesses a student's risk profile (Low, Medium, High, Critical) based on incident frequency and severity. 

By calculating recommended next actions and behavioral trends, the application empowers administrators to intervene constructively. A centralized grid features search, pagination, and status filters (All, Active, Completed, Archived). A robust reports dashboard visualizes performance and incident trends over time, providing a legal audit trail of all disciplinary actions and communications.
